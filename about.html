About us Sir Enoch Anyanwu Memorial convent

Sir Enoch Anyanwu memorial convent Umuariam Obowo is the only effective female boarding school along Owerri-Umuahia express-way. we teach our students how to combine school activities with worshipping and serving God. Recently our school added some international curriculum to our national curricula, we offer IELTS and many more. We encourage you to join the moving golden training of Sir Enoch Anyanwu Memorial Convent Umuariam Obowo, God bless you.

This 2021/2022 graduating students will make the 29th graduation ceremony of Sir Enoch Anyanwu Memorial Convent. It was founded by late Sir Enoch Anyanwu under the Anglican communion Okigwe-south Diocese.

Our Mission is to give our students sound education, ability to defend their abilities outside and good Godly morals .

Our Vision is service to God and Humanity.

We also have very seasoned and qualified teachers who provide high quality music lessons in singing and training on a wide range of musical instruments.
